## Usage

1. Sign up for an account or log in
2. Grant permission to access your webcam
3. The app will analyze your facial expressions to determine your focus level
4. Music will be played based on your focus level - calming music for low focus and fast tempo music for high focus
5. You can view your focus history and analytics in the dashboard

## Project Structure

- `/src` - Source code
  - `/components` - React components
  - `/contexts` - React context providers
  - `/hooks` - Custom React hooks
  - `/pages` - Page components
  - `/services` - Service modules for API calls
  - `/types` - TypeScript type definitions
  - `/utils` - Utility functions
